Mechanical Repair Services

Engine Repair, Overhaul, and Rebuild

Engine Repair, Overhaul, and Rebuild: We specialize in diagnosing and repairing engine issues caused by overheating, oil leaks, or internal wear. Our team can handle anything from minor repairs to complete engine rebuilds, restoring your engine’s performance and reliability.

Overheating Diagnosis & Repair

Overheating Diagnosis & Repair: Overheating can result from radiator faults, water pump issues, or low coolant levels. We inspect the entire cooling system, identify the root cause, and repair or replace faulty components to prevent further damage.

Transmission Repair & Replacement

Transmission Repair & Replacement: Whether you’re experiencing slipping gears, delayed shifting, or fluid leaks, our experts use advanced diagnostics and repairs to restore smooth transmission performance.

Manufacturer-Recommended Services

Manufacturer-Recommended Services: We follow manufacturer guidelines to perform regular maintenance, ensuring your car complies with warranty requirements and maintains peak performance.

Brake Repair & Replacement

Brake Repair & Replacement: Our brake services address issues such as worn pads, warped rotors, or hydraulic leaks, ensuring your vehicle’s safety through precise inspections and reliable repairs or replacements.

Suspension System Repair

Suspension System Repair: If your ride feels rough or you notice uneven tire wear, we inspect shocks, struts, and springs to repair or replace damaged components, improving comfort and handling.

Shock and Struts Replacements

Shock and Struts Replacement: Worn shocks and struts affect vehicle stability and control. We replace these components to restore a smooth and stable driving experience.

Steering System Repair

Steering System Repair: Steering issues like difficulty turning or misalignment are resolved through comprehensive inspections and repairs of the steering column, power steering pump, and linkages.

Tire Repair and Replacement

Tire Repair and Replacement: Whether it’s a puncture or low tread, we repair or replace tires with high-quality options, ensuring safety and performance.

Wheel Alignment & Balancing

Wheel Alignment & Balancing: Proper alignment and balanced wheels enhance driving stability, reduce vibrations, and extend tire life. Our advanced alignment machines ensure precise adjustments.
